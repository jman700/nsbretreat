The House

Adjust the thermostat to your preferred temperature. Keep all windows and doors closed — if left open for more than a few minutes, the AC system will shut off until they are closed again.

We provide complimentary coffee. The super-automatic espresso machine on the counter does everything — add beans and press your selection. Fill the water reservoir before brewing.

The Nespresso Original machine uses Original pods. Extra pods are available at the nearby Publix.

Additional coffee makers are in the pantry attached to the kitchen.

Add ice to the ice reservoir at the top. Add your mixer to the blending jar below. Press the blend button for perfect frozen drinks. Enjoy responsibly!

The kitchen is fully equipped with: refrigerator, stove/oven, microwave, dishwasher. Dishwasher detergent is under the kitchen sink. Trash bins are in the pull-out drawer to the left of the pantry. Trash bags are under the sink.

Washer and dryer are in the laundry room. Detergent is provided. Please keep load sizes reasonable. Wash pool towels and colored items separately from white linens.

Every bedroom and living space has a Smart TV. Netflix, Hulu, YouTube TV and more are available to download. Sign in with your own account. Please sign out before you leave.

The game room is upstairs. It features retro arcade games and a professional air hockey table. Games should be ready to play — if any have power issues, check that they are plugged in at the back of the unit.

The zero-gravity massage chair is in the living area. Use the remote to select a program. Recline using the footrest lever on the side. It's one of our favorite things in the house — enjoy it!

Pool & Hot Tub: In the backyard. See the Hot Tub section for startup instructions.

Putting Green: In the backyard. Putters are available — enjoy!

Covered Patio: Rear of the house with seating. Great for evenings.

Front Porch: Pull up a beach chair and watch the neighborhood.

Basic cleaning tools (brooms, mops, vacuum) are in the laundry room or garage. Cleaning supplies and dishwasher detergent are under the kitchen sink.

Please only flush toilet paper. Do not flush wipes, feminine products, or anything labeled "flushable" — this causes clogs. If you experience a plumbing issue or leak, contact Antonio immediately.

Hot Tub

The hot tub may need 1-2 hours to reach temperature. Start it early!

  1. 1
    Locate the hot tub control panel on the side of the tub.
  2. 2
    Press the Jets button to activate the jets. The pump will start.
  3. 3
    Set your temperature using the up/down arrows. We recommend 100–104°F.
  4. 4
    Replace the cover and allow 1-2 hours to heat if starting from cold.
  5. 5
    When finished, press Jets to turn off and replace the cover to retain heat.

Beach Tips

  • 🏖️ Beach gear is in the garage — chairs, umbrella, towels, cart, toys, and sporting equipment
  • 🚲 Bikes are in the garage — lock code is 81518
  • 🐢 NSB is a sea turtle nesting area — do not disturb nests or use bright lights near the beach at night
  • 🐕 Dogs are allowed on the beach — please clean up after your pet
  • 🍺 No glass containers on the beach
  • 🚗 Beach parking can be limited in peak season — biking or walking is recommended
  • 🌊 Always check surf and weather conditions before entering the water
  • ☀️ Florida sun is intense — reapply sunscreen every 90 minutes
