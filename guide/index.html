The House

Park in the right garage spot, driveway, or front yard grass. Please watch for the water meter and plumbing sticking out of the ground near the grass area.

Street parking is prohibited and may result in fines or towing.

If parking in the grass across the street, ensure your vehicle faces traffic flow (front toward the neighborhood interior, away from Atlantic Ave).

Your key is the black smart lock on the front door — no physical key needed.

  • To unlock: Press the house icon (bottom left), enter your code, and the door will unlock.
  • To lock: Press the lock icon (bottom right) when leaving.

Your door code will be sent to you in the messages section of your Airbnb or VRBO booking app prior to your arrival. If you have any trouble, call Antonio right away.

Please inspect the home when you arrive and notify us immediately if anything needs attention. If you notice any damage, document it with photos and send them via text or through the booking app.

Adjust the thermostat to your preferred temperature. Keep all windows and doors closed — if left open for more than a few minutes, the AC system will shut off until they are closed again.

We provide Folgers ground coffee — no need to bring your own. Available brewing options on the counter and in the pantry:

  • Drip coffee maker — use the provided Folgers grounds
  • French press — in the pantry
  • Bialetti Moka Express — stovetop espresso, in the pantry
  • Super-automatic espresso machine — uses whole bean coffee only. We recommend Nicaraguan beans from Island Roasters Coffee Company, or illy Classico from Publix. Add beans and press your selection; fill the water reservoir before brewing.
  • Nespresso Original — uses small Original pods. Extra pods available at Publix.
  • Keurig — in the pantry. Bring your own K-Cups or pick some up locally.

Add ice to the ice reservoir at the top. Add your mixer to the blending jar below. Press the blend button for perfect frozen drinks. Enjoy responsibly!

The kitchen is fully equipped with: refrigerator with ice maker, stove/oven, microwave, dishwasher. Dishwasher detergent is under the kitchen sink. Trash bins are in the pull-out drawer to the left of the pantry. Trash bags are under the sink.

Ice maker: The refrigerator ice maker is sized for normal family use. Larger groups may want to pick up extra bags of ice for coolers and entertaining.

The grill runs on the home's natural gas line — no propane tank to deal with. Follow these steps:

  1. Find the mechanical gas timer to the left of the grill behind the small stainless steel square access door. Open the door and twist the timer to start gas flow.
  2. Inside the cabinet under the grill, confirm the shut-off knob is in-line with the hose (gas on). If the knob is turned sideways, gas is off and the grill won't light.
  3. Light and use the grill as normal.
  4. When the timer runs out, gas shuts off automatically. For extended grilling sessions, simply twist the timer again to add more time.
  5. When finished, turn the timer off to stop gas flow.

Hot Tub

The hot tub, bubbles, and pool/spa lights are all controlled from the tablet mounted on the wall in the kitchen. Each function runs on a 3-hour timer — reactivate it if you need longer. Want it pre-heated before you arrive? Just message us and we'll set it up.

  1. 1
    Touch the tablet screen to wake it, then slide your finger upward from the bottom to unlock.
  2. 2
    Tap "815 NSB Pool System" to open controls.
  3. 3
    Select the "OneTouch Scenes" tab and toggle on "Spa Mode." The system will begin heating after a few minutes.
  4. 4
    Allow approximately 30 minutes to reach 102°F depending on starting temperature and weather.

Important: Under the Devices section, "Spa Mode" must be toggled on — not just "Spa Heater." Using only "Spa Heater" will not heat correctly.

On the tablet: select the "Equipment" tab, then toggle on "Air Blower."

On the tablet: select the "Equipment" tab, then toggle on "Pool Light."

When finished, please turn off heating to avoid unnecessary gas usage: select the "OneTouch Scenes" tab and toggle off "Spa Mode."

The sliding glass doors to the pool have a legally required audible alarm — a grey rectangular box to the left of the door. Please confirm it is turned on when you arrive.

  • To exit: Press the mute button before opening the door.
  • To re-enter: Press the small button on the outside of the door frame before opening.
  • The mute lasts 10–15 seconds — if the door stays open longer, the alarm will sound.
  • If your group is adults only, you may disable the alarm using the black switch on the left side of the device. Please do not disable if children are present.

Beach Tips

  • Beach gear is in the garage — chairs, umbrella, towels, cart, toys, and sporting equipment
  • Bikes are in the garage — lock code is 81518
  • NSB is a sea turtle nesting area — do not disturb nests or use bright lights near the beach at night
  • Dogs are allowed on the beach — please clean up after your pet
  • No glass containers on the beach
  • Beach parking can be limited in peak season — biking or walking is recommended
  • Always check surf and weather conditions before entering the water
  • Florida sun is intense — reapply sunscreen every 90 minutes
