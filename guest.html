Your key is the black smart lock on the front door — no physical key needed.

  • To unlock: Press the house icon (bottom left), enter your code, and the door will unlock.
  • To lock: Press the lock icon (bottom right) when leaving.

Your door code will be sent in the messages section of your Airbnb or VRBO booking app prior to arrival.

Park in the right garage spot, driveway, or front yard grass. Watch for the water meter near the grass area.

Street parking is prohibited and may result in fines or towing.

Please inspect the home when you arrive and notify us immediately if anything needs attention. Document any pre-existing damage with photos and send via text or booking app.

The sliding glass doors to the pool have a legally required audible alarm — a grey rectangular box to the left of the door.

  • To exit: Press the mute button before opening the door.
  • To re-enter: Press the small button on the outside of the door frame before opening.
  • The mute lasts 10–15 seconds.
  • If your group is adults only, you may disable the alarm using the black switch on the left side. Do not disable if children are present.

House Rules

Please be mindful of neighbors. Quiet hours apply to outdoor spaces — pool area, patio, and yard.

The property is permitted for a maximum of 16 guests at any time, including day visitors.

Use the driveway, right garage spot, or front yard grass only. Street parking may result in fines or towing.

Broken glass on outdoor surfaces is nearly impossible to fully clear and poses a serious injury risk — especially in and around the pool.

If you need to smoke, please step off the property and dispose of cigarette butts responsibly. Thank you for your understanding.

Pets must be pre-approved before arrival. Please clean up after your pet at all times.

Parties or events require prior written approval from the host.

Overnight guests must not exceed the booked headcount. No unregistered guests permitted to stay the night.

Scented, pillar, tea lights, and similar candles are a fire hazard. Birthday candles are fine. Battery-powered candles are always welcome.

Accidents happen — please notify Antonio right away rather than after checkout. Call or text (407) 506-6654.

Please roll bins to the curb by 7 AM and return them the same day.
