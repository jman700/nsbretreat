Your key is the black smart lock on the front door — no physical key needed.

  • To unlock: Press the house icon (bottom left), enter your code, and the door will unlock.
  • To lock: Press the lock icon (bottom right) when leaving.

Your door code will be sent in the messages section of your Airbnb or VRBO booking app prior to arrival.

Park in the right garage spot, driveway, or front yard grass. Watch for the water meter near the grass area.

Street parking is prohibited and may result in fines or towing.

Please inspect the home when you arrive and notify us immediately if anything needs attention. Document any pre-existing damage with photos and send via text or booking app.

House Rules

  • Quiet hours outside: 10 PM – 9 AM. Please be mindful of neighbors.
  • Max occupancy: 16 guests.
  • No street parking. Use the driveway, right garage spot, or front yard grass only.
  • No glass outside. Broken glass near the pool poses a serious injury risk.
  • No smoking on the property. If you need to smoke, please step off the property.
  • Pets require a $395 fee and must be pre-approved. Please clean up after your pet.
  • No parties or events without prior written approval from the host.
  • No unauthorized overnight guests beyond the booked headcount.
  • No open-flame candles indoors — fire hazard. Battery-powered candles are always welcome.
  • Report any damage immediately. Call or text Antonio at (407) 506-6654.
  • Trash pickup: Monday & Thursday mornings. Roll bins to the curb by 7 AM and return them the same day.

Pool & Spa

  1. 1
    Touch the tablet screen to wake it, then slide your finger upward from the bottom to unlock.
  2. 2
    Tap "815 NSB Pool System" to open controls.
  3. 3
    Select the "OneTouch Scenes" tab and toggle on "Spa Mode."
  4. 4
    Allow approximately 30 minutes to reach 102°F. Or — use the live controls below.

Important: Under Devices, "Spa Mode" must be toggled on — not just "Spa Heater."

The sliding glass doors to the pool have a legally required audible alarm — a grey rectangular box to the left of the door.

  • To exit: Press the mute button before opening the door.
  • To re-enter: Press the small button on the outside of the door frame before opening.
  • The mute lasts 10–15 seconds.
  • If your group is adults only, you may disable the alarm using the black switch on the left side. Do not disable if children are present.
